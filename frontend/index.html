Stock Market Overview

What is this?
This is an innovative stock market simulator designed to give you the thrill and experience of trading stocks without the risk of losing real money. In this simulator, you can buy and sell stocks just as you would in the real stock market, tracking your portfolio's performance over time. It's an excellent way to practice trading strategies, understand market dynamics, and compete with friends to see who can maximize their virtual profits. Whether you're a novice trader or an experienced investor, this simulator offers a risk-free environment to hone your skills.

How do I get started?
Getting started is simple and straightforward. Click on the "Get started now" button located above to begin your journey. You'll be directed to the dashboard where you can create a new account and dive into the world of stock trading. The dashboard also features a leaderboard where you can compare your performance with other traders and see how you rank. Start trading today and challenge yourself to become the top trader in the simulator!

How does it work?
To help you understand how the simulator functions, we've included a sample chart below showcasing the stock performance of Apple Inc. (AAPL). This chart is powered by the robust Polygon.io API, which provides accurate and up-to-date stock price data. The chart displays the closing price of the stock for each trading day over the last 30 days, giving you a clear view of its recent performance. By analyzing such charts, you can make informed decisions and develop effective trading strategies within the simulator.
